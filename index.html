Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your product lifetime predictions?

Our predictions are based on manufacturer data, usage patterns, and aggregated data from thousands of users. While no prediction is 100% accurate, our system typically achieves 85-95% accuracy for most consumer products.

Can I track multiple products at once?

Yes! Our platform allows you to track unlimited products across various categories. You can organize them by room, priority, or custom categories.

How does the chatbot determine product lifetimes?

Our AI chatbot uses a comprehensive database of product specifications, user reviews, and manufacturer data. It also takes into account common usage patterns and environmental factors to make accurate predictions.

Can I export my product data?

Absolutely! You can export your data in CSV, PDF, or JSON formats at any time for your records or to share with others.
